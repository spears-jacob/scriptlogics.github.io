Data Services

Data is complicated. Every system, methodology, and tech stack brings with it various benefits and challenges. On top of that, your organization's needs will be different from everyone else, depending on your budget, infrastructure, ingest and extract needs, on-hand talent, and myriad other factors. We help you to identify what are the most important goals for your team, and what tools, practices, and strategies will be most effective at achieving them.

We like to think of data as a life cycle in 3 parts:

  1. ORI: Organization, Research, and Ingest
  2. POP: Pipelining, Operation, and Production
  3. OCP: Optimization, Consumption, and Presentation

ORI Services and questions we can help answer: (Organization, Research, and Ingest)
  • Organization
    • Data Migration
      • System conversion (one warehouse to another).
      • Cloud migration (On-Prem to Cloud).
    • ETL or ELT?
      • How you intend to consume your data will inform which works best for your organization.
    • Schema
      • Is your data relational?
      • Is a file-distributed system best for you?
    • Platform
      • Do you already have a platform that you need help setting up?
      • Are there budgetary or organizational limitations to consider in choosing a platform?
      • What system is the most efficient for storing and organizing your data?
    • Architecture
      • Designing the Ingest process (how are you receiving your data?)
      • Building your Data Models to fit your reporting structure.
      • Creating a Data Map for your organization.
